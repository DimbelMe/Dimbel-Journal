---
title: A New World
---
I woke up alone, in a strange place.

A shack or shed of sorts, a broken house.

There was nothing and no one there, it almost looked burnt down.



As I got up and dusted my clothes, I realized how dark it was inside. There were naught but a few rays of sun illuminating the inside.



I walked towards the broken door and pushed on it with my body, as it seemed like it wouldn't open.



The door buckled with a loud creak, it bursted open as i fell onto the ground. I expected to get hurt from the fall, but the long grass below dampened my fall.



The strong sunlight blinded me shortly, and as I cleared my vision, I realized; I'm no longer home.



A tender yet deep purple sky, two suns on the horizon and a beautiful light covered the long and thin grass that extended through the land for miles.
