Let them wonder for years to come what my seemingly non-sensical letters meant, let them guess and second guess themselves as they traverse the deep mazes of my brain. 
It's meant for one and only one person to understand, and that person is neither me nor them. My muse and love, my true soul. They will never see you as i do, but this is the last time i talk with you. This is our last letter, an infinite amalgamation of words and sentences neatly stacked in a verse or a poem. This is not a letter saying goodbye, today i apologize, for i will leave you cold and lonesome as i cannot write again.
Maybe someone else will find you, my true soul, and give you the warmth you deserve. Maybe they'll do what i couldn't, and keep their spark alive while wandering the never ending halls of inspiration that you provide. Maybe they'll keep their sanity intact or lose it as i have, but none of that concerns me anymore, i shall leave this mortal coil for i can't face you from now on. It's up to them to figure out what i meant, it's up to them to judge and scrutinize every word that i have plastered in this white paper on my desk. It's up to them to decide if they should bury or burn my body once they find it. 
It's up to them, I've done my part.
