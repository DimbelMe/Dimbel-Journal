---
title: Help
---
"Help!"

A scream echoed

"Please, help me"

Her voice hurting my soul

"I don't want to die..."

After a loud noise the factory fell silent once more.



I've had been days on the expedition

Days of torture and pain

I killed more than a dozen creatures but this one, this one I didn't dare to even look at.

I found the old warehouse next to the lumbermill, where I fought for my life against a mutated. 

The size of me, with a bigger build and a putrid smell.

I managed to dodge the damage but I couldn't stay there for long.

The sound of battle and the scent of blood was bound to draw more of them to the area.

A few days later I found myself in this factory, an old ammo manufacturing company that had disappeared as soon as the apocalypse started.

I couldn't find much, everything was looted, burnt down or had been destroyed and taken apart for scraps.

Even then I managed to find one thing in the basement.

It was a blast door, covered in ash and dust, with a clear blast mark on the bottom part as if someone tried to use explosives to get inside. 

The poor bastard clearly had no idea what a blast door was.

From inside I heard a knock that made me almost pass out. Then another and a scratch.

And the voice of a little girl followed.

"Help!"

But I knew.

"Please help me"

But I could hear it.

"Please, let me out, mister!"

The distortion in her voice made me tremble, even with a gun in hand I felt weak and scared. 

I wasn't ready for whatever was on the other side. It made me nauseous and anxious, my every fiber fighting against it's existence.

I placed a makeshift claymore in front of the door. I'm no soldier, but I was taught by one. 

Place it, run like hell, get the button for the door... Boom.

A hellish scream that turned into a screech, a sound that sent chills down my spine even when I had resolved to see this to the end.

I checked the door. Blood and flesh all around. I checked inside, blood and flesh... All around.

Piles of ripped clothing and bones. Skulls littered around the room, ten, twenty, maybe more.

A feeding ground and a nest. Dozens of eggs. A breeding ground for the pests and monsters that had destroyed the world. 

A single flame was enough, a simple plan. I went outside the room, as it lit up. Probably traces of gunpowder that helped it burn.

It was done, so I went back home. I had found some food and a gun to boot, I was satisfied enough and wanted to rest. 

A few days back, a few rough nights. Once I reached camp, and finally managed to shut my eyes, I was tired, but I swear, I could still hear the voice that begged me... "Help"
