---
title: Rotten Fate
---
In between the dark alleys, overshadowed by the bright sky scrapers, there is a man who runs away. 

From his life, his mistakes, and his wife. From his past, his family, and the pain. 

The pain that he feels as an addict, because it's the only thing that calms him down.

 After witnessing a massacre from the quiet lips of the rich, after seeing his friends and family die, after gang violence took the future he had. 

He runs, past it all. Because there is nothing left behind for him to run back. 

His wife, his daughter, his mom and his dad. 

Hanging from the bridge as a sign. 

A sign that he refused. A sign that he saw as a challenge. 

A challenge to take back what he had, what he lost, what was owed.

But he won't, for he has no one and nothing left. 

Nothing but the tremble in his hands as he takes one last draw of the only constant thing throughout his life. 

The drug that will numb him down, that will make him feel like tonight is the night, the night he ends it all. 

Yet, it won't be. Cowardice and fear, the reminder of what he lost, the need for something stronger to quiet down his thoughts. 

It all started wrong, from the moment he was born in that childhood home. 

The home where he grew up and lived out his teenage years.

The home he was to inherit and restore.

The home he was planning to live with his family in.

A place he could never escape from, for it was all he knew.
