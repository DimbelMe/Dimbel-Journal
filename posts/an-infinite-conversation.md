---
title: An Infinite Conversation
---
I see it - *She says* - I see the golden light. The true finale of life, the end of the tunnel.

Welcome - *She hears* - A strange voice. Unknown yet soothing.

Welcome - *It continues* - Once again, to my world.

Where am I? - *She asks. With a saddened voice, and trembling hands.* - Who are you?

Who am I? - *It responds -* That's not quite right. The question is; Who are we?

Wait, we? - *She asks confused -* I hear only one voice, not two nor more. I hear only yours.

My voice is not mine - *It responds gently* -It's ours. A voice in an ocean filled with countless others.

We are infinity - *It continues* - But you... You hear only mine? That's a first, even for us.

But it's true - *She answers back* - I hear only one. Only yours.

Am I dead? - *She asks.*

As much as it pains us - *The voice responds* - That's also true. You are dead, dear. Have been for a while.

If I'm dead - *She says* - Are you death? Perhaps God? Is this heaven or hell?

This could be hell, for all I care - *She continues -* I don't deserve heaven. Nor do I care for it.

You are sad - *It answers back* - You are sad and lost. You were good in life. Albeit with some mistakes.

But even then - *The voice goes on* - You are incorrect. This isn't heaven nor hell. And we are neither God, nor death.

We are infinity - *It continues* - We are what was, what is, and what will be.

But then - *She interrupts* - That means you are me. So how could I exist if you have always been me?

You are confused - *It answers gently* - And I understand. You are unique, not a part of us.

You are your own being - *It continues* - And that's why you are here. You can choose to go, or you can stay, besides me.

I can stay? - *She asks* - I can stay with you, infinite well of knowledge...

I'm already dead - *She goes on -* So what does it matter, whether I stay or not?

I'd rather get to know more. More of you, infinity.
