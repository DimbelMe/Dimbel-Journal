title: Reflection
---
There's an extensive ocean that covers the horizon. The sun is setting and painting the ocean with a beautiful orange. The sky is clear and open, and there's a soft breeze that wanders on the coastline.

There's a man, all alone. Talking to himself on the pier.

What happened to us? - *Asked the man -* I was sure that I made you happy. I did my best, for us. For our family. I was happy, you know? I always was. Waking up next to you, holding you in my arms as we fell asleep. 

I know. - *A woman said -* I know you were. And so was I. Being with you, and our daughters, I was as happy as anyone could ever be. But I've lived a hard life, you know that. I didn't expect it, either. It was something that came out of the blue, but then again, I know I'm at fault.

You are not at fault, my love. - *Answered the man, with a tender voice* - You are not at fault. I know you've had a tough life. Your depression and everything that your family did. I understand, and I don't blame you.

I miss you, you know. - *He continued* - I've missed you so much. Our daughters is all grown up now, and she doesn't blame you, nor resent you. She loves you as much as I do. 

I never expected that you would take your life that night. It was such a beautiful night...
