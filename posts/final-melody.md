---
title: Final Melody
---
I heard it once, as a child. 

That perfect melody from a pair of beautiful hands, a man who didn't play the piano but the piano followed his hands. 

Music itself was shy. And he showed me how to fight that, that night. 

His music was perfection, it was soothing and regal. It was a beautiful and enchanting melody that I have forever pursued. 

I'm all grown up, I've seen 30 some winters, I've lived chasing music with an unwavering passion. 

I've written and performed melodies that made Kings and Queens cry. 

I've lived like I've always wanted to, and yet I'm not alive. I can never be, until I find that melody. 

The melody that haunts me to this day, for it's unobtainable beauty...

I heard it once, as a child. I heard it twice, when my parents died. 

And I hear it again, as my life flashes before my eyes, as this knife pierces my heart, as I see myself die.

I was never close to it, now I realize. 

That melody haunted my life and it still haunts me as I die. I know this is the end, and I want it to be this way.

That melody, that dreadful, haunting yet beautiful melody, that was nothing but my final breath.
