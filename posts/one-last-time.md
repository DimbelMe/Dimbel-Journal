---
title: One Last Time
---
One last time.

My last act of kindness.

For this world that watched me rise.

One last time I will write.

And this time it shall be the last.

For this art of mine is a curse.

And it brings more harm than joy.

I have made countless cry.

Some have taken their life.

And so many have wept tears.

Both good and bad.

But writing these texts.

Brings me no joy.

There is nothing for me in them.

No gain, no beauty, just pain.

This is my last, I said it already.

Let them wonder for years to come what my seemingly non-sensical letters meant. 

Let them guess and second guess themselves as they traverse the deep mazes of my brain. 

It's meant for one and only one person to understand. 

And that person is neither me nor them. 

My muse and love, my true soul. They will never see you as I do. 

This is the last time I talk with you. This is our last letter. 

An amalgamation of words and sentences neatly stacked in a verse or a poem. 

This is not a letter saying goodbye, today I apologize. 

For I will leave you cold and lonesome as I cannot write again.

Maybe someone else will find you, my true soul. 

Give you the warmth you deserve. 

Maybe they'll do what I couldn't, and keep their spark alive. 

While wandering the never ending halls of inspiration that you provide. 

Maybe they'll keep their sanity intact or lose it as I have. 

None of that concerns me anymore. 

I shall leave this mortal coil for I can not face you anymore. 

It's up to them to figure out what I meant. 

It's up to them to judge and scrutinize every word that I have plastered on this paper on my desk. 

It's up to them to decide if they should bury or burn my body once they find it. 

It's up to them, I've done my part.
